About

TSEC

The Thadomal Shahani Engineering College was founded in 1983. It is the first and oldest private engineering institute to be affiliated with the University of Mumbai, one of the oldest universities in Asia. TSEC ranks within the top 1% of India's private engineering colleges and has, for 31 years, formed the perfect amalgamation of academia and culture.

MS-TSEC

At the Management Society (MS), we believe in being the difference. This belief drives us to be the difference between a degree and an education. Through the conceptualization of various initiatives that range from debates to quizzes that draw the best minds from across the nation, we strive to engineer experiences that educate and empower the youth of today and the leaders of tomorrow.
