Letter from Honorary Secretary General

Dear Delegates, Faculty Advisors and OC Members
It brings me great pleasure to be a part of TM MUN 2015 in an advisory capacity. Having founded TM MUN in 2011, it is one of those conferences which have stood the test of time and quality and offers one and all a plethora of opportunities to debate, deliberate, document while broadening their scope of thought.

With a range of different committees which offer several different options for discussion, I am sure that the MUN is going to be a wonderful platform for the youth to express themselves and learn new things about the rest of the world.

At TM MUN, we focus on ensuring that each and every one of the participants receives an equal opportunity to participate to the best of his/her abilities.

I urge all delegates to come well prepared for the conference and wish you all the best with the entire process that a MUN comprises.

I look forward to meeting all of you at TM MUN 2015.


Regards,
Anansh Prasad
Honorary Secretary General
